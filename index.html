Here are the answers to the 5-mark questions: # Importance of Value Education Value education is essential for individuals to develop a strong moral compass, enabling them to make informed decisions and lead a purposeful life. It helps individuals understand and appreciate the importance of values such as respect, empathy, honesty, and responsibility. Value education fosters personal growth, promotes social cohesion, and contributes to the development of a more compassionate and just society. # Salient Values of Life Some of the salient values of life include: 1. Respect: Valuing the dignity and worth of every individual. 2. Empathy: Understanding and sharing the feelings of others. 3. Honesty: Being truthful and transparent in all interactions. 4. Responsibility: Taking ownership of one's actions and their consequences. 5. Compassion: Showing kindness and concern for the well-being of others. # Requisites of Interpersonal Skills Effective interpersonal skills require: 1. Active listening: Paying attention to others and understanding their perspectives. 2. Clear communication: Expressing oneself clearly and respectfully. 3. Emotional intelligence: Recognizing and managing one's own emotions and those of others. 4. Empathy: Understanding and sharing the feelings of others. 5. Conflict resolution: Managing disagreements in a constructive and respectful manner. # Role of Media in Value Building Media plays a significant role in shaping values and influencing behavior. It can: 1. Promote positive values: Highlighting stories and characters that exemplify positive values such as kindness, honesty, and respect. 2. Raise awareness: Educating the public about social issues and promoting critical thinking. 3. Provide role models: Offering positive role models and inspiring stories that promote values such as perseverance and resilience. 4. Foster empathy: Sharing stories and experiences that promote understanding and empathy towards others. 5. Encourage critical thinking: Promoting media literacy and critical thinking skills to help individuals evaluate information and make informed decisions.
